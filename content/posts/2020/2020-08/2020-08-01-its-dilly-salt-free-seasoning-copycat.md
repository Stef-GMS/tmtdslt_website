I ❤️ dill! Years ago I found a salt-free seasoning by McCormick called It’s Dilly. I was in heaven.

Then, like all good things, it came to an end. McCormick quit making it. 😭

In 2017 I stumbled onto a copycat recipe which is close. So I tweaked it a bit and love it. Happiness again! 😃

**It’s Dilly Salt-Free Seasoning Copycat**  
Servings and Yield:  
1/4 Cup = 12 tsp = 96 pinches = 192 dashes  
Assumes:  
1 tsp = 8 pinches OR 1 pinch = 1/8 tsp  
1 tsp = 16 dashes OR 1 dash = 1/16 tsp

![](/images/2020/2020-08/2020-08-01-its-dilly-salt-free-seasoning-copycat-img-1.jpg)**Ingredients**  
¾         Cup dry dill weed (not dill seed)  
½         Cup dry minced onion (not onion powder)  
⅓         Cup dry parsley  
¼         Cup dry basil  
¼         Cup dry tarragon  
2½       Tablespoons garlic powder  
1          Tablespoon finely ground black pepper  
1          teaspoon dry lemon peel  
           or 2 packets TrueLemon crystalized Lemon packets

**Directions**

1.  Combine all the ingredients in a small bowl and mix well. If you prefer more of a powder, place ingredients into Vitamix Dry container, food processor, spice mill, or use mortar/pestle to grind into a powder.
2.  Store in airtight container
