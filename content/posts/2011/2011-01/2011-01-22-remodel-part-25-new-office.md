Many have asked me what Phase 3 is… well, see those stairs, newel post and railing? We’re 98% sure that we’re moving forward with having those all replaced. _Hubby_ really wants to finish off the house with hard wood, well at least the traffic areas (OK, I mentioned it first)… so the carpet twill be torn out on the stairs and in the loft space at the top of the stairs. The new floor will be hardwood just as is shown in the picture above. Additionally, we will be having new trim put around all the loft-side of doorways and window to match the Phase 1 and 2 trimwork. There are still some things we need to discuss with Brent before we’re positive we’re doing it… but I will keep you all posted.
