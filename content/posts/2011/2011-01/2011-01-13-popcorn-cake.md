Cast of Characters (sorry, I forgot to get a photo):  
4 qts Popped popcorn (I use air popped)  
1.5 C salted peanuts  
10 oz peanut butter chips  
        and/or 16 oz Reeses Pieces  
        and/or 16 oz M&Ms  
1/2 C butter or margarine or Smart Balance (I use Smart Balance)  
1/2 C oil (I use light olive oil)  
16 oz mini marshmallows

Pre-grease pan (either an angel food or bundt or two loaf pans). As you can see, I chose to do two loaf pans.  
![wpid-IMG_6763-2011-01-12-00-01.jpg](/images/2011/2011-01/2011-01-13-popcorn-cake-img-1.jpg)

In a large bowl, mix popcorn, peanuts and peanut butter chips and if desired Reeses Pieces or M&Ms.  
![wpid-IMG_6761-2011-01-12-00-01.jpg](/images/2011/2011-01/2011-01-13-popcorn-cake-img-2.jpg)

Combine butter/margarine, oil and marshmallows in pan. Heat until marshmallows are melted.  
![wpid-MeltMarshmallows-2011-01-12-00-01.jpg](/images/2011/2011-01/2011-01-13-popcorn-cake-img-3.jpg)

Pour melted marshmallow mixture over popcorn mixture. Stir/fold until popcorn is well coated.  
![wpid-MixInMeltedMarshmallows-2011-01-12-00-01.jpg](/images/2011/2011-01/2011-01-13-popcorn-cake-img-4.jpg)

Pour contents into pan(s). Let Cool. Cover cake after cool.  
![wpid-IMG_6778-2011-01-12-00-01.jpg](/images/2011/2011-01/2011-01-13-popcorn-cake-img-5.jpg)
