I was born and raised in Kansas City, Missouri. It’s “home” to me… even though I have not lived there for 29 years… 🙁 [Recent storms](http://TooMuchToDoSoLittleTime.com/2011/05/praying-for-joplin-mo/) which have hit Joplin, MO tug at my heart strings so much.

A friend of mine sent me a link to the amazing photo of KC during the recent storm which rolled through the area. The first thing I thought of was “Ghostbusters”.

It turns out the photographer, Scott Cook, posted the image to a local news channel’s web site and it has gone viral since. Wlll, Mr. Cook is doing something AWESOME… he’s selling 8×10 photos for $15.00 and 11×14 for $20.00 with all the money going to _Joplin, MO Tornado Recovery_. The photo was taken from the top of Bishop-McCann on May 24, 2011.

Well, I didn’t want to steal the photo so I wrote the him and asked for permissions to post his picture here. He said yes. I am also posting instructions on how you can get your own copy and help out the people of Joplin, MO at the same time.

[![](/images/2011/2011-05/2011-05-27-kansas-city-storm-pic-to-raise-money-for-joplin-img-1.jpg "Duck and Cover")](http://contests.nbcactionnews.com/engine/Details.aspx?p=A&c=27808&s=8634568&i=1)

Used with permission of Scott Cook; taken from the top of Bishop-McCann on May 24, 2011
