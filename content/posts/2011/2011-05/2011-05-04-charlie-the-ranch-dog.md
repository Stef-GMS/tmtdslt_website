![wpid-book_clipart_2-2011-03-10-02-01.jpg](/images/2011/2011-05/2011-05-04-charlie-the-ranch-dog-img-1.jpg)If you’re a Pioneer Woman fan you are likely to know that she has another new book out. It’s called _Charlie the Ranch Dog_. Do you have your copy? It available on [Amazon](http://www.amazon.com/Charlie-Ranch-Dog-Ree-Drummond/dp/0061996556), [Barnes and Noble](http://search.barnesandnoble.com/Charlie-the-Ranch-Dog/Ree-Drummond/e/9780061996559/), [Walmart.com](http://www.walmart.com/ip/15228269) as well as other sites. If you’re into audiobooks, it’s less than $2.00 on [Audible.com](http://www.audible.com/pd/ref=sr_1_1?asin=B004WFXARO&qid=1304521562&sr=1-1).

I LOVE, LOVE, LOVE [Pioneer Woman](http://ThePioneerWoman.com)! I [met her](http://TooMuchToDoSoLittleTime.com/2010/04/meeting-pioneer-woman-a-dream-come-true/) over a year ago. What a wonderfully nice woman.

I have her other two books, [The Pioneer Woman Cooks: Recipes from an Accidental Country Girl](http://www.amazon.com/Pioneer-Woman-Cooks-Recipes-Accidental/dp/0061658197/ref=sr_1_3?s=books&ie=UTF8&qid=1304523200&sr=1-3) and [The Pioneer Woman: Black Heels to Tractor Wheels–A Love Story](http://www.amazon.com/Pioneer-Woman-Black-Tractor-Wheels--/dp/0061997161/ref=sr_1_1?s=books&ie=UTF8&qid=1304523200&sr=1-1).

This new book is a cute little read for kids. The story is about Charlie, her basset hound. I LOVE, LOVE, LOVE Charlie! 😀 I couldn’t wait for this book. I absolutely love the artwork. The story is geared toward kids, so adults may be bored, but I enjoyed it. I’ll give it 4 out of 5 stars – “really liked it”.

But guess what!?! Barnes and Noble has an [online version](http://www.facebook.com/l.php?u=http://www.barnesandnoble.com/u/online-storytime-books-toys/379002381/&h=e3584) with Ree reading it to us, too! I’m not sure how long it’s available. It’s about 7 minutes long… CUTE!

_I just love Charlie and the chipmunk is too cute! Who would of thought Ree had a pet chipmunk! 😉_
