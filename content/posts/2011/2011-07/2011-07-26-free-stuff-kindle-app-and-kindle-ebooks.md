![](/images/2011/2011-07/2011-07-26-free-stuff-kindle-app-and-kindle-ebooks-img-1.png "wpid-FreeStuff-2010-12-3-11-00.png")I recently stumbled across some [free Kindle ebooks](http://www.amazon.com/b/ref=amb_link_355500722_42?ie=UTF8&node=2245146011&pf_rd_m=ATVPDKIKX0DER&pf_rd_s=left-1&pf_rd_r=196W7K5SJD3GEA6BE0E4&pf_rd_t=101&pf_rd_p=1309893442&pf_rd_i=133141011). I’ve mentioned that I use Apple’s eBook reader, but I thought “what the heck” why not take a look. So I downloaded the [free Kindle app](http://www.amazon.com/gp/feature.html/ref=sa_menu_karl3?ie=UTF8&docId=1000493771) for my Mac and also the one for my iPhone (there is also one for iPad, Windows PCs, Blackberry, Android, and Windows 7 phone).

I know it seems odd for someone who doesn’t read a lot (I listen to books, remember) to get an eBook reader, but what the heck… it’s FREE and so were the books.

Since I registered my apps with my Amazon account, I now get Amazon Kindle emails. Had been ignoring them – again, not a “reader”; I’m a “listener”. Then one eBook caught my eye…

![](/images/2011/2011-07/2011-07-26-free-stuff-kindle-app-and-kindle-ebooks-img-2.jpg "wpid-IMG_1256-2011-03-14-00-01.jpg")If you recall, way back in March I talked about [My Favorite Love Story](http://TooMuchToDoSoLittleTime.com/2011/03/my-favorite-love-story/) and how I have several copies of _Pride and Prejudice_, but I didn’t own [Pride and Prejudice and Zombies](http://www.amazon.com/Pride-Prejudice-Zombies-ebook/dp/B004HW7E6U/ref=sr_1_2?s=digital-text&ie=UTF8&qid=1311432877&sr=1-2) by Jane Austen and Seth Grahame-Smith.

Well… Back in March _Pride and Prejudice and Zombies_ Apple eBook was $10.39 and I believe the Kindle eBook was around $13.00. In my opinion, too much to spend on an ebook I didn’t know if I _really_ wanted it.

Well, guess what?! Right now you can get the book for 99 cents (until July 27 [over 900 Kindle books are on sale](http://www.amazon.com/gp/feature.html/ref=amb_link_355831402_1?ie=UTF8&docId=1000705681&pf_rd_m=ATVPDKIKX0DER&pf_rd_s=left-1&pf_rd_r=1STZY15G5Z0ZVRG5RGEN&pf_rd_t=101&pf_rd_p=1309893422&pf_rd_i=1286228011))! YEP! Over 92% off!!! I now own the Kindle version and have it on my Mac and if so inclined can **easily** download it to my iPhone.

Once I get the book read, I’ll let you know what I think… in my ideal world… the Kindle app would read it to me… 😉 (who knows it may, but I don’t think so – [Amazon.com](http://Amazon.com) if you’re reading this… that would be really cool feature!!! 😉 )
