![](/images/2011/2011-07/2011-07-23-i-havent-forgotten-about-you-img-1.jpg "wpid-Overwhelmed-2011-07-23-01-02.jpg")Have you ever felt like you’re missing out on life? Me, too.

I feel really awful… My day is not be complete until I read all “my favorite blogs”, but I am sooo far behind that I feel like I’m missing out on so much.

Life has been nuts for so many months that I can’t believe it goes by so fast. I miss reading them. I had one that I read – and loved – and she turned her blog “private” and now I can’t read it. Oh well… I suppose if I look at it as the “glass if half full” instead of “glass is half empty”… it’s one less blog I need to get caught up on.

So if you read my blog and have noticed that I’ve been missing from yours – **I’m sorry!** I _really_ missed you and your stories.

I am trying to get caught up. I have all of them as RSS feeds on my computer – so I may not make it to your site, but I am reading them. I am trying to leave comments because I know we all LIVE FOR COMMENTS and I know how disappointing it is when you get “pageviews”, but no comments… It’s the story of my blog life! 🙁

I’m sorry – just know I’m still here and doing my best. I WILL GET CAUGHT UP! 😀
