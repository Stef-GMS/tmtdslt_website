I had my second harvest yesterday…

I bought [Ed Hume](http://www.humeseeds.com/) radish seeds and chose the [Sparkler](http://www.humeseeds.com/rdshsp.htm) and [Champion](http://www.humeseeds.com/rdshchmp.htm) radishes. They are SOOOO easy to grow. WOW! It’s been four weeks since I planted my radishes and look at these beauties!

Aren’t they beautiful?!

I need to buy more seeds so I can get another crop going.

Now, I just need my tomatoes to ripen and I’ll be set … I don’t plan on making cheese, but fresh veggies… Oh yeah!
