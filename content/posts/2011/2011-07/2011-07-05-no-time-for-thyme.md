In my _[Advice Needed: 2011 Garden, Part 1; what to plant](http://TooMuchToDoSoLittleTime.com/2011/07/advice-needed-2011-garden-part-1-what-to-plant/)_ post I showed you my garden. I forgot to share something…  
![wpid-StefsGarden2011-0627-labeled-2011-07-1-00-01.jpg](/images/2011/2011-07/2011-07-05-no-time-for-thyme-img-1.jpg)

See the “Thyme”? I’ve had that plant since the summer of 1992! It has been quite hardy… even neglected and survived. I’ve even thinned it a couple of years ago by giving some to _Ma_ and my friend Dude. It is still pretty big.

Here’s what it looked like before I trimmed it this year. I’m sure someone is going to say “don’t let it go to flower”… I don’t care… I let it go to flower every year… I love the flowers and wanted to share with you.
