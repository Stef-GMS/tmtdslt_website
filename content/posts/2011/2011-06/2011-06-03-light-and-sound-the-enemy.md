I suffer from migraines. For years I used to get them four times a week – back when I worked for Intel. After I was laid off from there back in 2006 they started to go away. Even when on unemployment for five months I didn’t have them as bad. Well, they’re coming back. Life has been really stressful of late and I right now I just want to throw up. I’m writing this with my eyes closed because the light from my laptop hurts. At least my Mac’s keys don’t “click click click” like when I’m using an external keyboard because sound is also an enemy right now.

I can’t take my prescription migrate medicine because I can’t drive for six hours after taking it… I’m drinking some tea to try to relax me so I can finish getting ready for work… which I’m late for which means I have to work late … ugh… more stress.

If this doesn’t make sense, blame the headache…

I sure hope this headache goes away because I’m going to the Burno Mars concert tomorrow night…
