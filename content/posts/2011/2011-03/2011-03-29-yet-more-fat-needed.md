It’s been a week since I started keeping a [food diary](http://TooMuchToDoSoLittleTime.com/2011/03/keeping-a-food-diary/) again. The only significant change in my diet is that I’m keeping track of what I eat and as a result I have found that I am not getting _enough fat_! So, I added more fat and I have lost 6.5 pounds so far! 😀

Here is what I ate for breakfast and lunch yesterday. Seems boring when I see it on “paper”…  
![wpid-Mar28BfastLunch-2011-03-29-06-30.jpg](/images/2011/2011-03/2011-03-29-yet-more-fat-needed-img-1.jpg)

I did get hungry and Dr. Oz says don’t let yourself stay hungry. I was also low in fat, so I had 16 almonds as a snack. I felt better and had a bowl on homemade chili for dinner. Then I made a [new banana bread](http://TooMuchToDoSoLittleTime.com/2011/03/banana-bread-with-bourbon/ ) and had a slice. Yummm.
