A while back I was catching up on reading at Sarah’s [blog](http://sarahvanderploeg.blogspot.com/) and I clicked on one of her “Link Within” links that led to her 2010 Valentine’s Day Dinner [post](http://sarahvanderploeg.blogspot.com/2010/02/v-day-dinner.html). She talked about making Pioneer Woman’s [Rosemary Rolls](http://thepioneerwoman.com/cooking/2009/10/buttered-rosemary-rolls/). They sounded quite yummy and reminded me I’d seen a variety PW’s posts about them so I decided to make them.

Pioneer Woman uses [Rhode’s rolls](http://www.rhodesbread.com/products/view/800). I had never used frozen rolls before. I didn’t expect to find the brand she uses here in Oregon (local readers… they’re at Winco). Go figure…

I was really surprised to see how well they rose after about 3 hours or so…  
![wpid-IMG_6995-2011-03-22-19-46.jpg](/images/2011/2011-03/2011-03-22-pioneer-womans-rosemary-rolls-img-1.jpg)

I used smart balance (instead of butter), rosemary from my herb garden and Kosher salt (instead of the coarse sea salt Pioneer Woman uses)…  
![wpid-IMG_6996-2011-03-22-19-46.jpg](/images/2011/2011-03/2011-03-22-pioneer-womans-rosemary-rolls-img-2.jpg)

They turned out light, fluffy and quite yummy!!!.  
![wpid-IMG_6998-2011-03-22-19-46.jpg](/images/2011/2011-03/2011-03-22-pioneer-womans-rosemary-rolls-img-3.jpg)
