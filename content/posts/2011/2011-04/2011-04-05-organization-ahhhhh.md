When I designed our [new kitchen](http://TooMuchToDoSoLittleTime.com/remodel-recap/) I wanted two things… lots of counter space and lots of storage…

I love the counter space…  
![wpid-BackofPeninsula-2011-04-5-00-01.jpg](/images/2011/2011-04/2011-04-05-organization-ahhhhh-img-1.jpg)

Strangely enough I feel like I don’t have enough cabinet space. I keep rearranging stuff. This is not too long after the kitchen was finished. Most of our dishes were Tupperware and plastic…  
![wpid-AboveRecyclingCenterBefore-2011-04-5-00-01.jpg](/images/2011/2011-04/2011-04-05-organization-ahhhhh-img-2.jpg)

We recently bought Fiestaware. LOVE IT! Put the Tupperware and plastic plates and bowls aside for our garage sale.  
![wpid-IMG_7240-2011-04-5-00-01.jpg](/images/2011/2011-04/2011-04-05-organization-ahhhhh-img-3.jpg)

We bought some new glass glasses and I wasn’t sure how they would fit in the cabinet. _Hubby_ went to _Storables_ and bought these nice little shelves and _Voila!_ an organized cabinet!  
![wpid-IMG_7246-2011-04-5-00-01.jpg](/images/2011/2011-04/2011-04-05-organization-ahhhhh-img-4.jpg)

_I feel better!_ 😀
