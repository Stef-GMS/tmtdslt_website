We had a visitor yesterday. _Ma_ and _Pa_ (our neighbors) were having a going away party and they needed someone to watch Rosie. So of course we said, “Yes.” 🙂

Well, as you can see… Rosie was a bit “home sick”… or at least wasn’t too keen on missing the party…  
![](/images/2011/2011-08/2011-08-15-home-sick-img-1.jpg)

After a while, she gave up (for a few minutes) and pouted on the couch…  
![](/images/2011/2011-08/2011-08-15-home-sick-img-2.jpg)

Boy, she was sure happy when it was time to go home… the party was still sort of going on…

_Sure hope she comes back for a visit again!_
