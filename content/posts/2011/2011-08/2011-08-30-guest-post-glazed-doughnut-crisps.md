Glazed Doughnut Crisps  
by [Sandra Lee](“http://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/sandra-lee/glazed-doughnut-crisps-recipe/index.html”)

*   Total Time: 1 hr 47 min
*   Prep 25 min
*   Inactive 1 hr 10 min
*   Cook 12 min
*   Yield: 16 cookies
*   Level: Easy

Ingredients
-----------

*   1 (18-ounce) package refrigerated sugar cookie dough, room temperature (recommended: Pillsbury)
*   1/2 cup all-purpose flour
*   1/2 teaspoon brandy extract
*   Prepared vanilla frosting
*   Drippy icing, recipe follows

Directions
----------

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

Knead dough with flour, adding in 2 parts, and brandy extract.

[![](/images/2011/2011-08/2011-08-30-guest-post-glazed-doughnut-crisps-img-1.JPG)](http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-45mSREyrnPc/TkvOiL9xJaI/AAAAAAAACn0/lfmxPZIHmhc/s1600/DSC_0346.JPG)

[![](/images/2011/2011-08/2011-08-30-guest-post-glazed-doughnut-crisps-img-2.JPG)](http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-Xp72EFseh6o/TkvOigj_tyI/AAAAAAAACn4/_bgCu6q7UVU/s1600/DSC_0352.JPG)

[![](/images/2011/2011-08/2011-08-30-guest-post-glazed-doughnut-crisps-img-3.JPG)](http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-VYYp_cJj9ts/TkvOi401XuI/AAAAAAAACn8/qRT2EjFKRTI/s1600/DSC_0355.JPG)

[![](/images/2011/2011-08/2011-08-30-guest-post-glazed-doughnut-crisps-img-4.JPG)](http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-FeHUelbeLKA/TkvPwVp60_I/AAAAAAAACoE/LmwKKiyLJ_o/s1600/DSC_0357.JPG)

Break into 2-inch pieces. Roll each piece into a 6-inch long rope. On an ungreased cookie sheet, shape rope like a doughnut, pinching ends together.

[![](/images/2011/2011-08/2011-08-30-guest-post-glazed-doughnut-crisps-img-5.JPG)](http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-NCgD0agH49Q/TkvPxqzI0XI/AAAAAAAACoQ/jDU0xGi9iCw/s1600/DSC_0362.JPG)

[![](/images/2011/2011-08/2011-08-30-guest-post-glazed-doughnut-crisps-img-6.JPG)](http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-4bkJb3HyYvQ/TkvPw64KZ6I/AAAAAAAACoI/02-qS4GeYTw/s1600/DSC_0360.JPG)

[![](/images/2011/2011-08/2011-08-30-guest-post-glazed-doughnut-crisps-img-7.JPG)](http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-Zy1P33ylAsM/TkvPxA2OK0I/AAAAAAAACoM/XVAsVqMJnPU/s1600/DSC_0361.JPG)

Freeze for 10 minutes. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes. Cookies should be just lightly golden on top.

[![](/images/2011/2011-08/2011-08-30-guest-post-glazed-doughnut-crisps-img-8.JPG)](http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-miFPEZpJsBw/TkvPyBdqu4I/AAAAAAAACoU/OL53L4_2xZ0/s1600/DSC_0364.JPG)

   
Cool completely. Frost each cookie with vanilla frosting. Drizzle Drippy Icing over cookies.

[![](/images/2011/2011-08/2011-08-30-guest-post-glazed-doughnut-crisps-img-9.JPG)](http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-DfZi6AcovvE/TkvPypNRX-I/AAAAAAAACoY/qcpzl2FCahQ/s1600/DSC_0382.JPG)

[![](/images/2011/2011-08/2011-08-30-guest-post-glazed-doughnut-crisps-img-10.JPG)](http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-YXzSJQUJAfc/TkvPy1csUqI/AAAAAAAACoc/p2Pk2rMtxU4/s1600/DSC_0385.JPG)

Drippy Icing:  
1 cup sifted powdered sugar  
2 tablespoons milk  
1 drop yellow food coloring  
In a small bowl, whisk together powdered sugar and milk.

[![](/images/2011/2011-08/2011-08-30-guest-post-glazed-doughnut-crisps-img-11.JPG)](http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-3kZ23R3HuJo/TkvPzYPpJQI/AAAAAAAACog/3__1z0zueq4/s1600/DSC_0388.JPG)

Tint with 1 drop yellow food coloring. (I left this step out…) Add more powdered sugar for stiffer consistency, if desired.

Drizzle over cookies.

[![](/images/2011/2011-08/2011-08-30-guest-post-glazed-doughnut-crisps-img-12.JPG)](http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-J-1q_OPvcFk/TkvPzw0TLUI/AAAAAAAACok/pSi7XipwOJU/s1600/DSC_0394.JPG)

[![](/images/2011/2011-08/2011-08-30-guest-post-glazed-doughnut-crisps-img-13.JPG)](http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-tVvoUbD0nBM/TkvP0KS_raI/AAAAAAAACoo/iVFej_3aI3U/s1600/DSC_0400.JPG)
