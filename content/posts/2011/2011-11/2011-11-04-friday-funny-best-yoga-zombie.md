I have mentioned my friend and co-worker _Dude_. _Dude_ is really into yoga. _Dude_ is really into dressing up for Halloween. _Dude_ is really into zombies. _Dude_ is a little nuts.

We had a Halloween contest at work and I bet you can guess _Dude_’s costume. Did you guess ‘zombie”? Good for you! 😉

I will warn you that if you’re not into zombies some of these photos may bother you… one of the ladies I work with literally ran away from him and hid behind a guy dressed up like a pirate! LOL!!

_Dude_ bought the makeup and another coworker created this masterpiece. Little did I know that _Dude_ wanted to enter a yoga Halloween costume [contest](http://www.yogadork.com/news/vote-for-your-fave-in-yd-halloween-photo-contest/). So please go vote for _Dude’s_ photo “_Zombiefied Cryptic Crow_” and help him beat out the Curious George/Man in Yellow Hat photo.
