_The Offspring_ and I are in the middle of watching _Eat, Pray, Love_. We just finished the _Italy part_… I now have the desire to escape the reality of my _real_ life and go to Italy for a year…

Wouldn’t it be fun to just escape and go to a far away place???

I’m so in love with Italian food and art…

Then I start thinking about all the things I need to do here… and there is no way I can take a year off from my life and go to Italy… oh well, it’s time for bed… perhaps I will dream of being in Italy… 😉

Tomorrow, we’ll finish the movie and I’ll see if I still have the same dreams…

Good night…

_Have you ever wanted to escape reality? If so, where do you want to go?_
