Cream Cheese Frosting, Reduced Fat  
——————————————————————————–  
Serving Size: 1  
Yield: 2.5 cups (120 1-tsp servings)

Ingredients:  
8 ounces reduced fat cream cheese (Neufchatel), softened  
2 teaspoons vanilla, clear  
1 teaspoon butter flavor, clear  
6 cups powdered sugar

Directions:  
1\. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese, vanilla and butter flavoring with electric mixer on medium speed until very smooth.

2\. Slowly beat in powdered sugar one half cup at a time. Beat until smooth.

3\. If desired, add your favorite food coloring. Mixing well until color is evenly distributed.

4\. Frost cake or cookies when cool.

5\. Refrigerate leftover frosting as well as cookies and cakes (due to cream cheese).
