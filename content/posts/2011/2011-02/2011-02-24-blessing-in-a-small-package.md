Today I spent time reminiscing…

…I thought about cottage cheese…  
![wpid-DSC00716-2011-02-24-22-00.jpg](/images/2011/2011-02/2011-02-24-blessing-in-a-small-package-img-1.jpg)

…I thought about how fast a tiny creature could eat her 2 tablespoons of cottage cheese… (for perspective… the bowls were about 1 cup)  
![wpid-Lady-CottageCheese-2011-02-24-22-00.jpg](/images/2011/2011-02/2011-02-24-blessing-in-a-small-package-img-2.jpg)

…I thought about how the little gem never complained when an eight-year-old dressed her up… put extra clothes on her to keep her warm…  
![wpid-DSC02092-2011-02-24-22-00.jpg](/images/2011/2011-02/2011-02-24-blessing-in-a-small-package-img-3.jpg)

…how she loved to be on our laps…  
![wpid-DSC02094-2011-02-24-22-00.jpg](/images/2011/2011-02/2011-02-24-blessing-in-a-small-package-img-4.jpg)

…Thinking how much I was in L-O-V-E when the tiny 1.2 pound puppy joined our family… so small that she fit in the palm of our hands… My foot was bigger than she was… Her new toy was bigger than her head… (oh… please don’t hold the hair and glasses against me… not to mention the shoes, blue socks… UGH, what _was_ I thinking??? Oh right … it was 1989!)  
![wpid-LadysAlbum-05-2011-02-24-22-00.jpg](/images/2011/2011-02/2011-02-24-blessing-in-a-small-package-img-5.jpg)

… the loves and kisses she used to give… oh yeah… and her tongue!  
![wpid-LadysAlbum-09-2011-02-24-22-00.jpg](/images/2011/2011-02/2011-02-24-blessing-in-a-small-package-img-6.jpg)

…the games we played… like _attacking Daddy’s slipper…_  
![wpid-LadysAlbum-12-2011-02-24-22-00.jpg](/images/2011/2011-02/2011-02-24-blessing-in-a-small-package-img-7.jpg)

_…and fetch with a pair of socks which were bigger than her…  
_![wpid-LadysAlbum-13-2011-02-24-22-00.jpg](/images/2011/2011-02/2011-02-24-blessing-in-a-small-package-img-8.jpg)

…she was more than a pet… she was a member of our family…

…Has it really been SIX-AND-A-HALF years!?!?! I still hear your bark and can feel your “neck hugs”… you brought so much joy to our lives… we were blessed to have you in our lives for 15 and 1/2 years… We miss you, _Lady_!!!

_Happy 22nd Birthday, Girl! Today I had cottage cheese in your memory…_
