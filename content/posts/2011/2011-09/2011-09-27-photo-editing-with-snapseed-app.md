Last week I saw a post on Twitter about receiving Nik Software’s [Snapseed](http://www.niksoftware.com/snapseed/) iPhone App for free (normally $4.99). [Nik Software](http://www.niksoftware.com) is well known for their photo editing software so I jumped on the free deal.

While on a daily walk with my friend _Dude_ I took this photo using Snapseed… Yeah, I love trees with the beautiful blue skies behind them… This photo is straight out of the camera (except for resizing and adding my copyright)…  
![wpid-Snapseed-Before-2011-09-27-06-45.jpg](/images/2011/2011-09/2011-09-27-photo-editing-with-snapseed-app-img-1.jpg)

While we were walking I fiddled with swipes and swishes… not really seeing what I was doing due to sunlight… but check out these results…  
![wpid-Snapseed-After-2011-09-27-06-45.jpg](/images/2011/2011-09/2011-09-27-photo-editing-with-snapseed-app-img-2.jpg)

Since we were walking, I am not 100 percent on all the effects, but I think these are all of them… “Drama” was first, then “Grunge” and finally “Organic Frames”.

I just wonder how great the photos will look when I am actually working at the photo editing. 😉 I think it is my new favorite iPhone photo editing app! (I just wish I had an iPad!)
