Pioneer Woman has a [photo assignment](http://thepioneerwoman.com/photography/2011/09/edit-this-4/) going this week. She wants us to edit [her picture](http://www.flickr.com/photos/pioneerwoman/6184222380/) of Charlie…

So, since [I am in love](http://TooMuchToDoSoLittleTime.com/2011/09/photo-editing-with-snapseed-app/) with my iPhone Snapseed app, so I decided to use my iPhone to edit the picture. Here is what I came up with… I titled this “Charle D’Grunge”…  
![Charlie D'Grunge](http://farm7.static.flickr.com/6158/6190895326_1c8f4209be.jpg)

The first thing I did was apply the Drama effect and bumped it up to 90%. Then I applied the Grunge effect and bumped it to 100%. The last effect was the Frames.
