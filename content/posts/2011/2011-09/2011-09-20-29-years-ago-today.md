Today is _Hubby_ and my 29th anniversary of our first date, a Chopin concert. You can read more about that night in my _[Fate post](http://TooMuchToDoSoLittleTime.com/2010/09/fate/)_.

We are college sweethearts. We’ve had people ask us how we’ve stayed together.

I won’t try to kid you… we are human and have our share of disagreements and arguments, but the key to our success is…

…we talk… we listen.

It may take a little bit of time, but that’s our “secret”. No matter what, we talk about whatever may be bothering us and we tell each other what is bothering us.

So… here’s to communication! _Cheers!_

_I love you, Hubby!_
