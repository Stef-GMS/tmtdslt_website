Why is it that my two most recent “LIFE” posts have had to do with crappy days at work? I had another crappy day at work. Another teary day I had. WHOA, I almost started sounding like [Dr. Suess](http://www.seussville.com/).

I don’t want to air my dirty laundry here because some people from work read my blog, but needless-to-say I was looking forward to my “Friday Cocktail”. 😉 Now, keep in mind I’m not a big drinker but have started enjoying a “Friday” (or Saturday) Cocktail.

I usually “steal” Emily or Jules’ cocktail recipes, but I don’t have any of [Jules](http://www.alittlebiteoflife.net/2011/10/halloween-cocktails-vampire-kiss.html)’ ingredients and [Emily](http://www.happyhomeblog.com/2011/10/28/hiya) has been buried at work so no cocktail this week. So, I walked into my pantry and grabbed some of my favorite liquors and stared at them. Decided they all sounded good and would likely make a fruity “numbing agent”.

So, as I sit back and watch the World Series, I’m SIPPING on my drink… any faster and I’d be out cold I think.

So without further delay…

Cast of Characters: Peach Schnapps, Pineapple Infused Vodka, Malibu Rum, Cointreau (Triple Sec), Citrus Infused Vodka, 6 oz can of Pineapple Juice, Shaker and Ice  
![wpid-photo-2011-10-28-19-01.jpg](/images/2011/2011-10/2011-10-28-friday-cocktail-end-of-a-crappy-day-img-1.jpg)

Put ice in a shaker. Add equal jiggers (1.5 oz) of each liquor. Add all 6 oz pineapple juice. Shake, pour and ENJOY!

_What is your Friday Cocktail?_
