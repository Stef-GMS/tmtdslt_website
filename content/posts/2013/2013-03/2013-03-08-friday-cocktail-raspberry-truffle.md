As the post title hints, OK it’s not really hinting, it’s called a _Raspberry Truffle_. The best part is that I get to use my new “cordial glasses” _Hubby_ gave me for Valentine’s Day! 😀

Cast of Characters: Cream (or half-in-half), Chambord (raspberry liqueur) and Amaretto. Remember you don’t have any Amaretto, can make _[Homemade Amaretto](http://toomuchtodosolittletime.com/2013/02/friday-cocktail-homemade-amaretto/)_.  
![wpid-RaspberryTrouffle-1-2013-03-8-15-45.jpg](/images/2013/2013-03/2013-03-08-friday-cocktail-raspberry-truffle-img-2.jpg)

Pour in Chambord into an ice-filled cordial glass, as you can see, I chose to not make mine “on the rocks”. Isn’t that a cool _cordial glass_? They can also be used for mini-trifles. 😉  
![wpid-RaspberryTrouffle-2-2013-03-8-15-45.jpg](/images/2013/2013-03/2013-03-08-friday-cocktail-raspberry-truffle-img-3.jpg)

Add Amaretto…  
![wpid-RaspberryTrouffle-3-2013-03-8-15-45.jpg](/images/2013/2013-03/2013-03-08-friday-cocktail-raspberry-truffle-img-4.jpg)

Top with cream…  
![wpid-RaspberryTrouffle-4-2013-03-8-15-45.jpg](/images/2013/2013-03/2013-03-08-friday-cocktail-raspberry-truffle-img-5.jpg)

Stir and garnish with raspberry or cherry if desired  
![wpid-RaspberryTrouffle-5-2013-03-8-15-45.jpg](/images/2013/2013-03/2013-03-08-friday-cocktail-raspberry-truffle-img-6.jpg)

I LOVE this drink! It’s tastes just like a raspberry truffle candy, but with a bit of a kick… YUMMMM!

> Recipe  
> ——————————————————–  
> 1 oz Chambord® raspberry liqueur  
> 1/2 oz amaretto almond liqueur  
> Cream (or half-and-half if that’s all you have)  
> Raspberry or Cherry for garnish  
> Ice if desired
> 
> 1\. Pour in Chambord into an ice-filled cordial glass
> 
> 2\. Add Amaretto
> 
> 3\. Top with cream
> 
> 4\. Stir and garnish with raspberry or cherry if desired
