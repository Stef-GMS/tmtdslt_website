In honor of St. Patrick’s Day this weekend, I thought I would share drink I found on Pinterest – actually the pin had [30 different shots](http://pinterest.com/pin/33706697183522937/). I chose the “Irish Flag” shot. The Irish flag is orange, white and green. So, this is a layered drink.

I’ve never done a layered drink before so it was as an interesting experiment. Also, the drink calls for “orange liqueur”. OK, I have both _Cointreau_ and Triple Sec and even the cheap Triple Sec isn’t “orange” colored, so I went with my favorite _Cointreau._

Cast of Characters: _Crème de Menthe_, _Irish Cream_ (_Bailey’s_), Orange Liqueur \[_Cointreau_ (or Triple Sec)\]  
![wpid-IrishFlagCast-2013-03-15-04-15.jpg](/images/2013/2013-03/2013-03-15-friday-cocktail-irish-flag-cordial-img-2.jpg)

Now, as I said, I have never made a layered drink, so please bare with me. 😉

Carefully pouring into cordial glass in the following order… _Cointreau_ (Triple Sec)  
![wpid-IrishFlag2-1-2013-03-15-04-15.jpg](/images/2013/2013-03/2013-03-15-friday-cocktail-irish-flag-cordial-img-3.jpg)

_Bailey’s_ Irish Cream  
![wpid-IrishFlag2-2-2013-03-15-04-15.jpg](/images/2013/2013-03/2013-03-15-friday-cocktail-irish-flag-cordial-img-4.jpg)

_Créme de Menthe  
_![wpid-IrishFlag2-3-2013-03-15-04-15.jpg](/images/2013/2013-03/2013-03-15-friday-cocktail-irish-flag-cordial-img-5.jpg)

Now, if you look closely at this photo, you can see the layering. As you can see, you really can’t see the “orange”, but I couldn’t bring myself to actually put food coloring in _Cointreau_. That would be so disrespectful of such a fine liqueur! 😉  
![wpid-IrishFlag2a-2013-03-15-04-15.jpg](/images/2013/2013-03/2013-03-15-friday-cocktail-irish-flag-cordial-img-6.jpg)

If you’re wondering what happens if you add the ingredients in reverse order, see how the drink on the right does’ t have the same size layer on top.  
![wpid-IrishFlag1and2-2013-03-15-04-15.jpg](/images/2013/2013-03/2013-03-15-friday-cocktail-irish-flag-cordial-img-7.jpg)

Even without the orange color… you could sure taste the orange flavor. This tasted AWESOME and will now be something I will enjoying making.

Gotta love, Pinterest! 😀

Irish Flag Cordial  
—————————————————————–  
Serving Size: 1 glass

Carefully layer in the following order…  
1 oz Cointreau (Triple Sec)  
1 oz _Bailey’s_ Irish Cream  
1 oz _Créme de Menthe_
