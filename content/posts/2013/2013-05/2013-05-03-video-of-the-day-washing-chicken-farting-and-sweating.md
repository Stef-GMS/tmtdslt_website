It’s amazing what you learn each day.

1) Don’t wash your chicken – washing causes bacteria to spray (fly/spread) around your kitchen. Cook to 165°F (74℃) to kill bacteria.

2) The average person farts 10 times a day. Healthy number is 10-20. More if eating “gassy” foods (garlic, onions, spicy foods, beer). Average volume = 0.5 qt to 1.5 qt per day. If no change in your life and you’re farting too much see your doctor.

3) Sweating cools your body. If you don’t sweat you can overheat; dress in light enough clothing.
