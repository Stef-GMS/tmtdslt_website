If you recall, I mentioned that on New Year’s Eve, _Hubby_ and I watched _[New Year’s Eve](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1598822/)_. Towards the end (1:29:42 into it), Sam ([Josh Duhamel](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0241049/)) arrives at the party and is handed his _Disaronno and Cranberry_ cocktail. Admittedly, I’d not heard of this drink before watching the movie. Since I like cranberry juice and amaretto, especially _Disaronno_, I wanted to try it and thought you might enjoy it,too.

Cast of Characters: Cranberry Juice, Disaronno Amaretto (almond liqueur) and ice.  
![wpid-IMG_3418-2013-01-18-13-30.jpg](/images/2013/2013-01/2013-01-18-friday-cocktail-disaronno-and-cranberry-img-2.jpg)

Put ice cubes into glass…  
![wpid-IMG_3419-2013-01-18-13-30.jpg](/images/2013/2013-01/2013-01-18-friday-cocktail-disaronno-and-cranberry-img-3.jpg)

Pour amaretto over ice  
![wpid-IMG_3420-2013-01-18-13-30.jpg](/images/2013/2013-01/2013-01-18-friday-cocktail-disaronno-and-cranberry-img-4.jpg)

Top off with cranberry juice and stir  
![wpid-IMG_3421-2013-01-18-13-30.jpg](/images/2013/2013-01/2013-01-18-friday-cocktail-disaronno-and-cranberry-img-5.jpg)

_Voila!_ You’re done. Yep, it’s really that simple… You can garnish with a slice of lime if you wish.  
![wpid-IMG_3422-2013-01-18-13-30.jpg](/images/2013/2013-01/2013-01-18-friday-cocktail-disaronno-and-cranberry-img-6.jpg)

If you don’t have any Disaronno on hand you have to go and get some. Yes, it’s a bit spender, but ohhhh it’s soooo worth it! 😀 Plus you’re going to need it… more amaretto recipes are coming. 😉
