I’ve had a couple of _Friday Cocktails_ which use _Frangelico_ hazelnut liqueur. I also LOVE amaretto which is an almond liqueur… like seriously LOVE it! I even have nonalcoholic amaretto flavored coffee syrup_._

When I stopped at the liquor store to buy more amaretto they had a sampling of _[Pisa Liqueur](http://www.liquorepisa.com)_. _Pisa_ is a delightful combination of hazelnut, almond and pistachio flavors which comes in a uniquely shaped bottle, as you can see, one that resembles the Leaning Tower of Pisa.  
![wpid-IMG_3383c-2013-01-11-08-40.jpg](/images/2013/2013-01/2013-01-11-friday-cocktail-pisa-liqueur-img-2.jpg)

I really am not sure I taste the pistachio… I don’t know if I’ve ever had pistachios… well, enough that I know the flavor… unlike almonds (amaretto). I can taste the amaretto.

I’ve had _Pisa_ “neat” and “on the rocks”. I prefer it on the rocks, but occasionally have it neat. Either way, it is quite tasty!  
![wpid-PisaNeatRocks-2013-01-11-08-40.jpg](/images/2013/2013-01/2013-01-11-friday-cocktail-pisa-liqueur-img-3.jpg)

_Have you had Pisa yet? Sounds good, eh? I totally recommend rushing out to get it right now._
