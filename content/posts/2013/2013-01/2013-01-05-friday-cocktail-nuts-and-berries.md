I have had several Friday Cocktails which have used vodka or other “hard” liquor. Today I decided I wanted something less strong. My _[Peppermint Froth](http://toomuchtodosolittletime.com/2012/12/friday-cocktail-peppermint-froth/)_ recipe has been quite popular on Pinterest so I thought I would make another cocktail using _Frangelico_. I’m sure Juile from _[A Little Bite of Life](http://www.alittlebiteoflife.net)_ will be happy as I know she loves _Frangelico_.

Cast of Characters: Half-and-Half, Chambord (raspberry liqueur) and Frangelico (hazelnut liqueur)  
![wpid-NutsAndBerriesCast-2013-01-5-08-30.jpg](/images/2013/2013-01/2013-01-05-friday-cocktail-nuts-and-berries-img-2.jpg)

To a frosted old fashioned glass, add a jigger of Frangelico (or other hazelnut liqueur)…  
![wpid-JiggarOfFrangelico-2013-01-5-08-30.jpg](/images/2013/2013-01/2013-01-05-friday-cocktail-nuts-and-berries-img-3.jpg)

…then add a jigger of Chambord (or other raspberry liqueur)….  
![wpid-JiggerOfChambord-2013-01-5-08-30.jpg](/images/2013/2013-01/2013-01-05-friday-cocktail-nuts-and-berries-img-4.jpg)

Next, slowly pour the Half-and-Half to fill glass…

Next, gently stir…  
![wpid-NutsAndBerriesFinal-2013-01-5-08-30.jpg](/images/2013/2013-01/2013-01-05-friday-cocktail-nuts-and-berries-img-6.jpg)

…and enjoy…

The nuttiness of the Frangelico mixed with Chambord’s raspberry flavor makes this a perfect after dinner drink.

Nuts and Berries  
——————————————————  
Yield: 1 glass

Ingredients:  
3 oz Half-and-Half  
1 1/2 oz Chambord (raspberry liqueur)  
1 1/2 oz Frangelico (hazelnut liqueur)

Directions:  
1\. To a frosted old fashioned glass, add a jigger of Frangelico (or other hazelnut liqueur)

2\. Add a jigger of Chambord (or other raspberry liqueur)

3\. Slowly pour the Half-and-Half to fill glass

4\. Gently stir and enjoy
