Did you have a safe and fun New Year’s Eve? _The Offspring_ went to a friend’s house and they watched movies and ate [Popcorn Cake](http://toomuchtodosolittletime.com/2011/01/popcorn-cake/).

_Hubby_ and I stayed home and played a Acey-Ducey (backgammon game) and watched [_New Year’s Eve_](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1598822/ "New Year's Eve") on cable. I hadn’t seen the movie before and _Hubby_ said that he liked it. I’m glad we did. I really enjoyed it.

Our timing was nearly perfect… when the Times Square ball was dropping in real life (OK, tape delayed for us here on the West Coast), it was just about to drop in the movie! Not sure if _Hubby_ planned it that way but it was really cool for me. 😀

I had suggested playing “strip” Acey-Ducey – so that you have to take a piece of clothing off when your checker gets bumped, but _Hubby_ said it was too cold… We were watching it in his “man cave” and he was right… it was cold. So, after I took my socks off I gave up and put them back on… he didn’t even try! LOL!

_Hubby_ requested my _[Pineapple Candy](http://toomuchtodosolittletime.com/2012/11/friday-cocktail-pineapple-candy/)_ drink…

For me, I fixed the drink I mentioned in my [Friday Cocktail – End of a Crappy Day](http://toomuchtodosolittletime.com/2011/10/friday-cocktail-end-of-a-crappy-day/) post.
