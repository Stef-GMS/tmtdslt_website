Well, it’s just about time for me to start unpacking… I found the following locally at the [Container Store](http://www.containerstore.com/shop/kitchen?N=6&Ntt=drawer+organizer+expand) and [Storables](http://www.storables.com/bamboo-expandable-utensil-tray.html).

I bought the bamboo, but am now having second thoughts. Bamboo or plastic?  
![wpid-Picture14-2010-12-5-01-40.png](/images/2010/2010-12/2010-12-05-need-advice-bamboo-or-plastic-img-2.png)![wpid-Picture5-2010-12-5-01-40.png](/images/2010/2010-12/2010-12-05-need-advice-bamboo-or-plastic-img-3.png)![wpid-Picture9-2010-12-5-01-40.png](/images/2010/2010-12/2010-12-05-need-advice-bamboo-or-plastic-img-4.png)

> They are both adjustable.
> 
> Bamboo closely matches the inside of the kitchen drawers.
> 
> Bamboo is a renewable resource.
> 
> Plastic is cheaper.
> 
> Plastic can be wiped with a wet cloth.
> 
> Plastic is white. Bamboo is… well… wood.
> 
> Bamboo can splinter, plastic can’t easily splinter…
> 
> Bamboo is quite a bit more expensive. The pieces shown are $29.95 (bamboo) and $17.99 (plastic).

_What do you think?_
