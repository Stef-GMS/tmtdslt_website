I’ve discussed my cookbook and Le Creuset addictions… I’m also addicted to Christmas music! I just bought ColdPlay’s _[Christmas Lights](http://www.amazon.com/Christmas-Lights/dp/B004EKNC7K/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&qid=1291402572&sr=8-1)_ and Owl City’s _[Peppermint Winter](http://www.amazon.com/Peppermint-Winter/dp/B004BUKP3C/ref=sr_shvl_album_1?ie=UTF8&qid=1291402489&sr=301-1)_ from Amazon. _Peppermint Winter_ clip cracked me up! 😆

> “I twirl through the driveway with angelic grace ‘Til I slip on the sidewalk and fall on my face”.

If you use an iTunes and haven’t ever bought from Amazon, take a look. Some songs are cheaper on Amazon than iTunes (both of the two songs above are $1.29 on iTunes and I paid $0.99 for them on Amazon).
