Thought of the Day…_There are four basic food groups: milk chocolate, dark chocolate, white chocolate, and chocolate truffles. ~Author Unknown_

_The Offspring’s_ good friend Sarah, whom I affectionately call _My Sarah_, came over for a visit not too long ago. I had promised her that whenever she came over next we would make cookies. _My Sarah_ said that she doesn’t do much baking, so I decided it was a good opportunity for the girls to make them on their own… I was nearby in case they ran into problems.

I remembered that a few weeks ago, Nicole over at [That’s Life](http://tnicolej.blogspot.com/) wrote about making _[Yummy Chocolate Chip Cookies](http://tnicolej.blogspot.com/2010/06/yummy-chocolate-chip-cookies.html)_. _My Sarah_ loves oatmeal cookies and can’t, like me, can’t have chocolate, so I modified the recipe (sorry, Nicole) and they made it with white chocolate chips instead.

I explained to the girls that it is important to make sure they have all the ingredients as well as the correct amounts. I suggested they get prep bowls out and pre-measure everything. They did and then started following the recipe.

I forgot one thing… _The Offspring_ hadn’t used my KitchenAid mixer. I forgot to explain turning it on slowly with dry ingredients and also to put on the pouring shield. Both of which are “second nature” (sort of) for me, but to the novice… not so much…
