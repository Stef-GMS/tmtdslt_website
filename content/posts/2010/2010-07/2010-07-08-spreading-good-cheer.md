Thought of the Day… _Today, give a stranger one of your smiles. It might be the only sunshine he sees all day. ~Quoted in P.S. I Love You, compiled by H. Jackson Brown, Jr._  
  
I love making people smile and bringing cheer to people’s lives… I’ve been away a while and I’ve missed writing my blog, but I’ve missed reading others’ blogs. I have been trying to get caught up.

Tonight I was reading [Nicole’s blog](http://tnicolej.blogspot.com/) and got quite a surprise. She gave me a “[Sweet Blog Award](http://tnicolej.blogspot.com/2010/06/omg-seriously.html)”.  
![blog-2010-07-8-23-17.jpg](/images/2010/2010-07/2010-07-08-spreading-good-cheer-img-1.jpg)

This is the kind of “award” that brings recognition to others’ blogs. It’s “going around” right now, but who cares! What means the most to me is that Nicole thought of my blog and I appreciate that. Thanks, Nicole!
