Thought of the Day…_In order to change we must be sick and tired of being sick and tired. ~Author Unknown_

On Friday, I took _The Offspring_ to the doctor… after being poked, prodded and X-rayed… we found out that she had early pneumonia. UGH!

…Her fever reached 103.2 on Saturday… her cough was worse… DOUBLE UGH! As a mom, I felt helpless…

…Sunday her temp stayed high… coughing and coughing… not able to sleep… again, I felt helpless…

Today, her temp is coming down and cough is still around, but there is a little light at the end of the tunnel…

…and I don’t feel quite so helpless… until she starts coughing again… then my heart aches…

Get well, SOON, _Offspring!_
