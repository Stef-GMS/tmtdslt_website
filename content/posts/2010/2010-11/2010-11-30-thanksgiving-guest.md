Went spent Thanksgiving with _Hubby’s_ parents and sister. While visiting we saw a young deer walking through their front yard.

He was hungry…  
![wpid-IMG_6104-2010-11-30-10-00.jpg](/images/2010/2010-11/2010-11-30-thanksgiving-guest-img-1.jpg)

Uh oh… he caught me looking at him. I froze in my place…  
![wpid-IMG_6110-2010-11-30-10-00.jpg](/images/2010/2010-11/2010-11-30-thanksgiving-guest-img-2.jpg)

He was there for nearly 30 minutes.  
![wpid-IMG_6116-2010-11-30-10-00.jpg](/images/2010/2010-11/2010-11-30-thanksgiving-guest-img-3.jpg)

Cute tongue shot…  
![wpid-IMG_6117-2010-11-30-10-00.jpg](/images/2010/2010-11/2010-11-30-thanksgiving-guest-img-4.jpg)

He didn’t seem to mind when I started moving….  
![wpid-IMG_6122-2010-11-30-10-00.jpg](/images/2010/2010-11/2010-11-30-thanksgiving-guest-img-5.jpg)

He came closer…  
![wpid-IMG_6123-2010-11-30-10-00.jpg](/images/2010/2010-11/2010-11-30-thanksgiving-guest-img-6.jpg)![wpid-IMG_6124-2010-11-30-10-00.jpg](/images/2010/2010-11/2010-11-30-thanksgiving-guest-img-7.jpg)

He heard a car door slam a few houses down…  
![wpid-IMG_6125-2010-11-30-10-00.jpg](/images/2010/2010-11/2010-11-30-thanksgiving-guest-img-8.jpg)

He decided he’d had enough pictures taken and proceeded up next to the house where he could have his privacy. 😉  
![wpid-IMG_6127-2010-11-30-10-00.jpg](/images/2010/2010-11/2010-11-30-thanksgiving-guest-img-9.jpg)
