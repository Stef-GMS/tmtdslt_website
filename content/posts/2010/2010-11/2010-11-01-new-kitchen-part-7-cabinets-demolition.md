Thought of the Day…_Is it over yet?_

As you know from reading my other [Kitchen Remodel](http://TooMuchToDoSoLittleTime.com/category/kitchen-remodel/) posts, I am _sooooo_ ready for my kitchen remodel to be over (and yet it really hadn’t even started).

I finished packing the kitchen last night and made it to bed at a reasonable hour, sort of. I was startled awake when I heard the newspaper being thrown onto the driveway and panicked that I’d overslept…

…I couldn’t figure out why it was so dark… I had pushed my sleeping mask off and then I looked at the clock… ARGH! It was 3:00AM! _Think I’m a little stressed?_

The _guys_ (Brad, who you met in my _[New Kitchen, Part 2: Contractors](http://TooMuchToDoSoLittleTime.com/2010/10/new-kitchen-part-2/)_ post, Kevin and Miguel) arrived promptly at 8:00AM, just as we’d arranged, this morning to start the kitchen demolition.

Brad had not seen the mold on pervious visits because he didn’t want to disturb the mold behind the poster board I had put up to “hide” it. As I stood across the room, he took off the poster board. _Hello, you ugly mess! Your days of making me sick are up! You ready to leave my house?_  
![wpid-IMG_5587-2010-11-1-20-151.jpg](/images/2010/2010-11/2010-11-01-new-kitchen-part-7-cabinets-demolition-img-1.jpg)

First to leave the room was the island… _Good-bye, Island_… Uh oh… the electrical wire was coming up in the middle. We’d all assumed it would be on the right… means it has to be moved. _Why would the builder have them put it on the end?_ He was an IDIOT!  
![wpid-IMG_5589-2010-11-1-20-151.jpg](/images/2010/2010-11/2010-11-01-new-kitchen-part-7-cabinets-demolition-img-2.jpg)
