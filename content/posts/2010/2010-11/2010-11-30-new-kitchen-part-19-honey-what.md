When you hear “_Honey Bucket_” what do you think?

Most of the world thinks this…  
![wpid-HoneyPot-2010-11-30-10-00.png](/images/2010/2010-11/2010-11-30-new-kitchen-part-19-honey-what-img-1.png)

But, here in Oregon…  
![wpid-IMG_6094-2010-11-30-10-00.jpg](/images/2010/2010-11/2010-11-30-new-kitchen-part-19-honey-what-img-2.jpg)

…this is what we think…  
![wpid-IMG_6096-2010-11-30-10-00.jpg](/images/2010/2010-11/2010-11-30-new-kitchen-part-19-honey-what-img-3.jpg)

Not so bad, right? 😉 Actually, since we haven’t had a toilet on the main floor of our house for three-and-a-half weeks, we’ve had a Honey Bucket in our driveway…  
![wpid-IMG_6097-2010-11-30-10-00.jpg](/images/2010/2010-11/2010-11-30-new-kitchen-part-19-honey-what-img-4.jpg)

It has come in handy… but I will admit that having the whole neighborhood aware of trips to the “little girls room” has been a bit embarrassing, but it’s better than having to use a real bucket in the driveway! 😆

Oh yeah… going to the _Honey Bucket_ in 41 degree weather… NOT FUN! 🙁

_Strange that I never feel embarrassed when I’m using one at a softball tournament. Maybe it’s because everyone else has to, too!_ 😉
