Greetings, All. Happy Tuesday! The sun is shining here in the Pacific Northwest and the blue sky is nice to see. Here’s the view outside my office window (yes, Boss-man, it’s lunch time so I’m taking a break from “slaving away at the computer”). Doesn’t that blue sky look GREAT?!

![photo.jpg](/images/2010/2010-05/2010-05-04-readers-from-around-the-world-img-1.jpg)

Yesterday I made the [comment](http://www.toomuchtodosolittletime.com/2010/05/another-dream.html) that I didn’t know how many readers I have. I still don’t know exactly how many because of RSS feeds, but I checked the statistics and have found that I have had 102 visitors, 78 of which are unique. That means that there have been at least 78 people out there who have read my blog. Needless-to-say, I was overjoyed. I have only received a few comments so I didn’t know who might be out there.

Most of the views were on April 30 (59 of them)! WOW! Views have come from all around the world!! I was stunned. Visitors came from the USA (26 of the 50 states so far), Canada, India, Philippines, Spain, France and Israel. Thank you all. I’d love to hear from you. Leave a comment and let me know where are from.

I’d love to hear from you… your thoughts on my posts… are they boring?

If you’re reading via an RSS feed (Mom, Sis, Rafael, you know I’m talking to you, right?) be sure to click on the link that takes you to the original item so you, too, will be counted… 🙂
