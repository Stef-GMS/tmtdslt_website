Welcome back!

As I mentioned in yesterday’s [post](http://www.toomuchtodosolittletime.com/2010/05/haunted-tree-part-1.html), I need your advice picking out the best photo effect. I used Pioneer Woman’s Photoshop [Actions](http://thepioneerwoman.com/photography/2009/08/pioneer-woman-action-sets-1-and-2-updated/) to get these effects.

I will often use the _Quick Edge Burn_ on photos. It gives it a more dramatic look and feel. So I thought I’d try it combined with some the other actions. When I first started this process I thought I knew which one I liked, but now… after looking at them… I do need your help…
