Thought of the day… _“Give your stress wings and let it fly away.” -Terri Guillemets_

Well… I’d love to find a way to give my stress wings. Clearly Terri Guillemets didn’t have my day job. I’m the only one who does what I do at my company and… well… it is often quite nice but other times … it can very ….Very …. VERY stressful.

Where can I find those wings?

I worked 30 hours in two days… with 4 hours of sleep… got a migraine that lasted all day… can’t come up with a more in-depth topic for today… I’m writing this while I eat my breakfast… oh…. it is lunch time… whatever…

I call this photo _I’m Melting_ (another one of my _Lost in Color_ photos)… it was me last night… I melted when I went to bed… ahhhh…. sleep… it’s a wonderful thing… then went back to bed after I took _The Offspring_ to school… ahhhhh….
