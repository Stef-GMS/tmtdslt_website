Well… now back to the end of the game… we WON! Final score 1-0.

Not only did we win… our pitcher had a PERFECT GAME!! For those who don’t know the details of softball (or baseball) this means 21 outs (softball plays seven innings) with no opposing team members reaching a base (either on error, walk or hit). Perfect Games are RARE. Our head coach has been coaching for 35 years and has only coached \[now\] THREE Perfect Games.

WOW!

The pitcher was so focused on doing her job that she didn’t realize that she had pitched a Perfect Game. As soon as the last out occurred _The Offspring_ was so excited for our pitcher because she knows how special it is… she is a former pitcher who had a near Perfect Game at age 10 (one runner reached first on a hit by pitch). _The Offspring_ ran to the pitcher and threw her arms around her and said, “You pitched a Perfect Game!” The pitcher looked surprised and then it registered.
