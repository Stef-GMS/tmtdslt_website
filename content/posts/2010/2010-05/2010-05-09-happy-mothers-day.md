_Remember that behind every successful woman……is a basket of dirty laundry. -Author Unknown_

I love that quote.

_Mama_, thank you for all the wisdom and knowledge you have given me. For teaching so very much – how to smile, laugh, cook, sew (albeit I’m not nearly as good as you), be independent, stand up for myself and most importantly how to love my child. I LOVE YOU, MOM!!!

_The Offspring_, I would much rather spend time with you than doing laundry or housework. You bring me so much joy and pride. Without you my life would be incomplete!! Thanks, _Sweetie_!!! I love you more than blueberry milkshakes!

_Hubby_, thank you for understanding how important that quote is, but most of all thank you for _The Offspring_.

_Mum_, thank you for _Hubby_!

_Linda_, thanks for everything you’ve done for me.

_Ma_, you may not be my blood Ma, but you’re definitely my Ma. Thank you for letting me take the fence down – it’s SOOOO MUCH easier getting to you now!! I’m sure Rosie likes it, too. 🙂

Happy Mother’s Day to all the moms out there!
