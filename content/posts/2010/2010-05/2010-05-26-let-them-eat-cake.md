Thought of the day…_”The belly rules the mind.”_ -Spanish Proverb

Another late night working (2am this time)… _The Offspring’s_ Round 2 game was rained out and rescheduled for tonight (Wed)… UGH!

Remember the _[Yellow Cake with Buttercream Frosting](http://www.toomuchtodosolittletime.com/2010/05/yellow-cake-with-buttercream-frosting)_ from a couple days ago? Well, it was a huge hit… _Hubby_ went to get a piece… it was all gone (remember it was a small cake – only 12 pieces).

So to give myself a 10 minute break from working… I made another one last night… increased the vanilla from 1/3 tsp to 1/2 tsp… Yummmy (I have updated the post from the other day)…

I didn’t frost it this time (to save calories)… ohhhhhh… it is soooo moist! It sticks to your fingers…

Anyway, you know the saying _Let them eat cake_ don’t you? Well… it is half gone already… (also gave a piece to our neighbors)…

WAIT…. I just went to take a picture for you to see… and look what I found … there’s another piece missing! Looks like I have a _cake bandit_!
