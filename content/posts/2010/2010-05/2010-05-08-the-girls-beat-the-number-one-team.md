_The Offspring’s_ softball team beat the number one team in our league tonight! With our win tonight we are 5-7. That’s AMAZING!!!! Last year we only won one league game and as far as I know it has been three seasons since they’ve had a such a good season.

At the beginning of the season everyone discounted our girls… they were wrong! Our new coach has worked the girls hard – 3 hour practices 6 days a week before the games started and on non-game days… but it has really payed off!!
