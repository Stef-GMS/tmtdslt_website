Thought of the Day…_Don’t be a wall-flower… get out there and mingle! ~Unknown_

![wpid-IMG_3404-2010-08-2-20-28.jpg](/images/2010/2010-08/2010-08-02-mingle-monday-img-1.jpg)

_What in the world does that photo have to do with “mingling”?_ you ask… this was on our [July 4th hike](http://TooMuchToDoSoLittleTime.com/2010/07/22805-steps/)… it was **REALLY HOT** and people were stopping wherever we could find shade and chat with one another… this was _mingling in the desert_!

For the last couple of week’s I have been attending [Life of Meg’s](http://megnificentlife.blogspot.com/) _Mingle Mondays_. It’s a chance to find new blogs and learn/see new things.

This week I started with Meg’s blog… Her cupcake photo made me really hungry!

…Then moved on to [Creative Flair](http://www.creativeflairart.com/). There I found a divine looking [Rice Krispie treat](http://www.creativeflairart.com/2010/08/rice-krispie-decadance.html). Can I hear … YUMMMMM?!? I kept looking around which led me to [Jen’s Ink Pen‘s](http://jeninkpen.blogspot.com/2010/07/blogging-etiquettea-fairy-tale.html) post on Goldilocks and Blogging Etiquette. It was a cute read. She also has several creative art objects to share; including an Esty shop.

…I stopped by [The apple doesn’t fall far…](http://www.applesfalling.com/) and she had some really cute photos and ideas for a baby shower. I LOVED the idea of a onsie and bib making station at the shower! 💡

…Next stop [Northwest Betty](http://northwestbetty.blogspot.com/)… I love her dog Dudley! I’d love to know if the Cowboy’s [birthday candles](http://northwestbetty.blogspot.com/2010/07/cowboy-turning-30.html) melted when they were put on the grill with the corn and steaks.

…Ended my reading with [Making the Most of Megan](http://missmegan-rn.blogspot.com/). She talked about her recent trip to a North Carolina vineyard and asked about our favorites wines. Sounds like she had a good time, except for the chiggers. I’m so glad we don’t have them in Oregon! 🙂

_Do you mingle on blogs?_ If so… why not share via the comments section some of the blogs you visit and why you like them – or don’t… We can all learn from your experiences. 🙂
