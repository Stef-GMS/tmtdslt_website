Thought of the Day…_Is it REALLY October 1??? Man, where’d the time go? ~Me_

Yeah, I know… this isn’t really a _Behind the Camera_ post… it’s more of a _In Front of the Camera_ post… (I think I said that the last time I posted a John T. Woods post.)

Remember my friend whose son is the actor? If not, take a look at my [John T. Woods](http://TooMuchToDoSoLittleTime.com/2010/08/john-t-woods/) post. Love his smile!!  
![wpid-Picture14-2010-08-14-16-16.jpg](/images/2010/2010-10/2010-10-01-john-t-woods-update-img-1.jpg)

I got an email from him and his _[NCIS](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0364845/)_ episode will be episode 6 and is set to air on **Tuesday, Oct 12 (8:00-9:00 PM, ET/PT) on CBS**. That’s less that two weeks!

I sure hope he doesn’t die or is the bad guy. 😀

He “dies well”. He was the victim when he was on _[The Forgotten](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1514365/)_ and died when he was on _[24](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1463812/)_. He was a “bad cop” when he was on _[Dark Blue](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1486916/)_. Come to think of it, he was a thief on _[iCarly](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1451617/)_.

In the meantime, his _[Boss of Me](http://sabipictures.com/bossofme/episode-1/)_ comedy web series is still going strong. John plays _Bret Donovan_ who is sent from “corporate” to be the _New Creative Director_ at the independent production company called Sabi Pictures. Sabi is a real production company and the people in the series are John’s friends. It’s a crack up!!

Don’t forget to set your Tivo (or other DVR) for _NCIS_!!
