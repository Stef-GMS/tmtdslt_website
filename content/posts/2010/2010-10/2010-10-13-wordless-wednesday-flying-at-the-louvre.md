I know it is supposed to _Wordless Wednesday_, but I can’t go without doing a note of explanation…

I cracked up when I saw this photo… It is of my co-worker Mike who just returned from a multi-week vacation to Paris where he enjoyed among other things visiting the [Louvre](http://www.louvre.fr/llv/commun/home.jsp?bmLocale=en).

When I see this photo, it reminds me that he is flying away on retirement next month. Unfortunately for me, he’s the primary user for one of the programs I support. 😥

I bet he’ll be enjoying his travels even more knowing he won’t have to come back to work when he gets _home_!

**Happy Retirement, Mike!!!** _(Are sure you don’t want to rethink your plans?)_
