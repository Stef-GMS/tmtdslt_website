Thought of the Day… _May the sun shine, all day long, everything go right, and nothing wrong. May those you love bring love back to you, and may all the wishes you wish come true! ~Irish Blessing_

![wpid-nightengalelamp-2010-10-19-06-25.jpg](/images/2010/2010-10/2010-10-19-three-wishes-img-1.jpg)

Someone asked me, _If you could be granted three wishes, what would they be?_ You know, that’s a really tough question…

What’s the first thing that pops into your mind?

I don’t know if could narrow it down to only three things…

…but for me, it wouldn’t _money_ or _fame_… perhaps…  
Less stress would be nice…  
Happiness for my family and friends…  
Cures for diseases… to name only a few… diabetes, cancer (all forms, not just one),  
All evil in the world to end… hatred, bigotry…

Why can’t we all get along like most of the people in _Star Trek_ world?

Yeah, I know there are those who we mean and violent, but all those people on the Enterprise D got a long quite well… OK, there were a few nut jobs… but for the most part… they all got along and had fun doing their jobs… and they didn’t even get paid for it… WOW what a life!

OH… lest we forget… they had cured _the common cold_ and headaches were unheard of… unless there was a Ferengi with a mind control machine that causes the worst headaches that even Dr. Crusher couldn’t fix.  
![wpid-Untitled-2010-10-19-06-25.jpg](/images/2010/2010-10/2010-10-19-three-wishes-img-2.jpg)

And then there’s _Q_… HE was a headache! Oh, but I loved the _Q_ episodes, didn’t you?!?! 😀
