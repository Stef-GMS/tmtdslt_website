Thought of the Day…_A bird doesn’t sing because it has an answer, it sings because it has a song. ~Maya Angelou_

When I went to get tomatoes for my lunch I heard a distinct chirping. I slowly turned around and there _she_ was… One of the hummingbirds which frequent our hummingbird feeder.

I went inside to get my camera… and quickly shot these out our kitchen window… sorry the quality and that blasted [dead tree](http://TooMuchToDoSoLittleTime.com/2010/04/no-time-to-breathe-or-is-it-eat-let-alone-yard-work/) is still there…

She sat there chirping…  
![wpid-IMG_4806-2010-09-9-00-01.jpg](/images/2010/2010-09/2010-09-09-chirps-and-other-things-img-1.jpg)

…watching me…

…waiting for me to get away from the window… (she wanted me out of the way so she could go to the feeder hanging on the window where I was standing)…  
![wpid-IMG_4808-2010-09-9-00-01.jpg](/images/2010/2010-09/2010-09-09-chirps-and-other-things-img-2.jpg)

…patiently waiting…  
![wpid-IMG_4811-2010-09-9-00-01.jpg](/images/2010/2010-09/2010-09-09-chirps-and-other-things-img-3.jpg)

…wanting privacy… (the blue from the _Birthday Greetings_ I wrote on the window for _The Offspring_)  
![wpid-IMG_4809-2010-09-9-00-01.jpg](/images/2010/2010-09/2010-09-09-chirps-and-other-things-img-4.jpg)

…Oooops! Didn’t meant to take _that_… Oh, how nature is funny… strange what you can capture with a camera…

What? Didn’t see it? Let me highlight it for you…  
![wpid-IMG_4809_2-2010-09-9-00-01.jpg](/images/2010/2010-09/2010-09-09-chirps-and-other-things-img-5.jpg)

Yep… I caught her pooping! “It” happens! LOL!!! I crack myself up sometimes! Please forgive me!

She just sat there chirping at me to get out of the window…  
![wpid-IMG_4810-2010-09-9-00-01.jpg](/images/2010/2010-09/2010-09-09-chirps-and-other-things-img-6.jpg)

…I obliged…
