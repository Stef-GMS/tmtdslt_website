Thought of the Day…_Parrots make great pets. They have more personality than goldfish. ~Chevy Chase_

Do you ever get frustrated with bell pepper seeds when you’re slicing and/or dicing a bell pepper? Do you start by cutting in and around the stem and then dealing with the leftover seeds? I used, too. But never fear… there’s an easier way!

Just like yesterday’s _[Cut onion without tears](http://TooMuchToDoSoLittleTime.com/2010/09/cut-onion-without-tears/)_ post, this is post is “prep” for a recipe I will be sharing in a few days. 😉

Before we begin, sharpen your knife. It is **very** important to always start with a sharp knife.

First cut off the stem  
![wpid-IMG_4843-2010-09-13-12-45.jpg](/images/2010/2010-09/2010-09-13-slice-bell-pepper-and-avoid-seeds-img-1.jpg)

Next stand the pepper on it’s top  
![wpid-IMG_4844-2010-09-13-12-45.jpg](/images/2010/2010-09/2010-09-13-slice-bell-pepper-and-avoid-seeds-img-2.jpg)

Now take your knife and slice inside each lobe, _around_ the seeds in the center  
![wpid-IMG_4845-2010-09-13-12-45.jpg](/images/2010/2010-09/2010-09-13-slice-bell-pepper-and-avoid-seeds-img-3.jpg)

Rotate pepper and continue slicing inside each lobe  
![wpid-IMG_4847-2010-09-13-12-45.jpg](/images/2010/2010-09/2010-09-13-slice-bell-pepper-and-avoid-seeds-img-4.jpg)![wpid-IMG_4848-2010-09-13-12-45.jpg](/images/2010/2010-09/2010-09-13-slice-bell-pepper-and-avoid-seeds-img-5.jpg)

When you are finished, the center will remain with the seeds “contained”. (sometimes I carefully cut the little bit of flesh remaining around the stem area)  
![wpid-IMG_4849-2010-09-13-12-45.jpg](/images/2010/2010-09/2010-09-13-slice-bell-pepper-and-avoid-seeds-img-6.jpg)

Toss the center into your compost pail and proceed with slicing and/or dicing. Be sure to slice and dice from the inside of the pepper and not from the outside.  
![wpid-IMG_4851-2010-09-13-12-45.jpg](/images/2010/2010-09/2010-09-13-slice-bell-pepper-and-avoid-seeds-img-7.jpg)![wpid-IMG_4852-2010-09-13-12-45.jpg](/images/2010/2010-09/2010-09-13-slice-bell-pepper-and-avoid-seeds-img-8.jpg)
