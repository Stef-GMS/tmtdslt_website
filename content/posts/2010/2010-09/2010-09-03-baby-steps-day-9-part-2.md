Thought of the Day…_As you simplify your life, the laws of the universe will be simpler; solitude will not be solitude, poverty will not be poverty, nor weakness weakness. ~Henry David Thoreau  
_  
If you recall from _[Baby Steps, Day 9, Part 1](http://TooMuchToDoSoLittleTime.com/2010/09/baby-steps-day-9-part-1/)_, I broke the day into two parts. I had a very successful day! 😀

As mentioned in _Part 1_, the _FlyLady says that **Baby Steps, Day 9**_ is “[Declutter](http://flylady.net/pages/FLYingLessons_Declutter.asp) a few minutes at a time”.

_Morning (Before Work) Routine_ – NO work today, so just what I did after I got up

1.  Get up and dressed – YEP
2.  Look at sticky notes in the kitchen and bathroom – YEP
3.  Put out ONE _Hot Spot_ for 2 minutes – YEP, for more than 2 minutes
4.  [5 minute _Room Rescue_](http://www.flylady.net/pages/FLYFaq.asp#rescue) – YEP, for more than 2 minutes

_During the Day Routine_

1.  Recognizing the negative voices and change them to positive. That is what _FLYing_ (**F**inally **L**oving **Y**ourself) is all about. – YEP
2.  Read [Big Tent](https://www.bigtent.com/groups/flylady) or [FlyLady.Net](http://FlyLady.Net) email messages
3.  Keep your sink shining – YEP
4.  Walk a minimum of 7,500 steps but goal is 10,000 steps – I was on my feet most of the day and walked quite a bit, but I didn’t wear my pedometer. I am going to take a “YEP” anyway. 😉

_Before Bed Routine_

1.  Keep your sink shining – YEP
2.  Put out ONE _Hot Spot_ for 2 minutes (The _FlyLady_ recommends doing this twice a day) – YEP, for more than 2 minutes
3.  Set out clothes for tomorrow – YEP
4.  Go to bed a decent hour – NOPE (can you tell from the time on this post 😉 LOL! _Hubby_ just called my cell phone and said, “Bed, NOW!”)
