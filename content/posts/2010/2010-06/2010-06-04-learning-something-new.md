Thought of the Day… _People learn something every day, and a lot of times it’s that what they learned the day before was wrong. ~Bill Vaughan_

Each day (OK, sometimes it’s a few days) I get a new reader… I’d love to learn more about my readers…

Please add a comment and answer the following:

1) How did you find my blog?

2) Where do you live?

3) What kind of topics do you like best? Cooking, photography, technology, general life, softball?

4) Do you blog? If so, what’s your blog address?

5) What’s your favorite blog? You know the one – the one you _have to read_ or your day isn’t complete.

Have a GREAT day!
