As I mentioned on Friday’s _[Out for Delivery](http://TooMuchToDoSoLittleTime.com/2012/03/out-for-delivery/)_ post, I was anxiously waiting for my iPad

I didn’t want to go anywhere to get my iPad, I waited “not so patiently” for FedEx to show up on Friday. The FedEx delivery arrived hours later than they usually show up. The driver looked frazzled and I said, “Rough day?” To which he replied, “It’s Christmas all over in the wrong season.” He was so right… I couldn’t wait for him to get there. Not having either of the first two generations I was quite anxious. My AppleTV arrives on Wednesday. It’s Christmas again this week. Poor guy!
