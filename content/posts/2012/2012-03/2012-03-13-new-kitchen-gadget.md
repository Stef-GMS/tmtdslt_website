I have often discussed my addictions ([Le Creuset](http://TooMuchToDoSoLittleTime.com/tag/le-creuset/), cookbooks, movies…). Well, have I mentioned I’m addicted to kitchen gadgets as well? _Mama_ is too and I think _Ma_ is too. Well, a few weeks ago I was in Kansas City and _Mama_, _The Offspring_, and my step dad hit TJ Maxx… Since I was flying back to Oregon I knew I couldn’t take Le Creuset, but I thought I would see what gadgets they had. (BTW, I DID Le Creuset… but it was for _Mama_. I needed to get her addicted like I did _Ma_… Oh I’m so BAD!)

Anyway, before I darted over to the Le Creuset area, I saw this cool Joseph Joseph Elevate Small Spatula. I grabbed it as soon as I saw it… _the hot pink was calling to me!_  
![wpid-IMG_0878-2012-03-13-16-00.jpg](/images/2012/2012-03/2012-03-13-new-kitchen-gadget-img-1.jpg)

I have several other [Joseph Joseph](http://www.josephjoseph.com/) gadgets so I know I’d be happy, but this one intrigued me.  
![wpid-IMG_0879-2012-03-13-16-00.jpg](/images/2012/2012-03/2012-03-13-new-kitchen-gadget-img-2.jpg)

It has a built-in stand (or “tool rest” as they call it) to keep the spatula off the counter! 😀  
![wpid-IMG_0883-2012-03-13-16-00.jpg](/images/2012/2012-03/2012-03-13-new-kitchen-gadget-img-3.jpg)

I haven’t seen it at any other store, but I haven’t really looked. I know Amazon has it [here](http://www.amazon.com/Joseph-Elevate-Small-Spatula-Pink/dp/B004UDZUFI/). Evidentally, there are other “Evelate” items! Come to think of it, I have some Joseph Joseph tools I haven’t shared yet…
