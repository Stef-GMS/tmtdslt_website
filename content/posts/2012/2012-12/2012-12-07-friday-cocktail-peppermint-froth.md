I jotted down the ingredients and decided I would attempt my own version.

Cast of Characters: Grenadine, _Frangelico_ (hazelnut liqueur), peppermint schnapps, vodka, cream (didn’t have cream so for my remake I used milk)  
![wpid-IMG_0888-2012-12-7-14-55.jpg](/images/2012/2012-12/2012-12-07-friday-cocktail-peppermint-froth-img-3.jpg)

For my remake, I put a jigger and a half of vodka and a jigger schnapps. All I had is _Rumple Minze_ which is strong (100 Proof) so I only put half a jigger. Add a splash of Frangelico and cream. Shake until frothy and then added a drizzle of Grenadine. Mine was pinker, but who cares… guess I didn’t get my drizzle right. Oh well; it was still YUMMY! 😀

Peppermint Froth  
—————————————————-  
Serving Size: 1 glass

1 ounce Peppermint Schnapps  
2 ounce Vodka  
1 splash Cream  
1 splash _Frangelico_ (hazelnut liqueur)  
1 drizzle Grenadine

1\. Pour vodka and schnapps to shaker.

2\. Add a splash each of cream and Frangelico.

3\. Shake until frothy.

4\. Drizzle Grenadine into mixture.

5\. Pour into glass and serve.
