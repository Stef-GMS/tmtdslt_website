WOW! Can you believe it?! Christmas is less than two weeks away! I can’t believe it. Where the heck is time going? Have you been busy shopping and planning your Christmas menus? I’m going to do some sampling this weekend to decide if I plan on adding something new to our Christmas Feast.

There are few blogs that I have been reading for years; one of which is by Jules at [A Little Bite of Life](http://www.alittlebiteoflife.net). A couple of weeks ago I received word from Jules that I won her “Green Giant Seasoned Steamers [Giveaway](http://www.alittlebiteoflife.net/2012/11/new-green-giant-seasoned-steamers-prize.html)”. I was excited because I haven’t won many of these blog drawings. She made my day!! Thanks, Jules!!!!

Well, my package arrived a few days ago. I cracked up when I found this HUGE box at the front door. I wasn’t expecting something so HUGE!  
![wpid-GreenGiantSteamersGifts-1-2012-12-13-07-30.jpg](/images/2012/2012-12/2012-12-13-gifts-from-jules-via-green-giant-img-1.jpg)

… then I opened it and found a “ton” of paper…  
![wpid-GreenGiantSteamersGifts-2-2012-12-13-07-30.jpg](/images/2012/2012-12/2012-12-13-gifts-from-jules-via-green-giant-img-2.jpg)

When I removed the paper, I found my goodies… I giggled, with glee partially because of how big a box they sent for these items… silly people! What you see here is on the left is an apron, then the white is a large bowl with a coupon for the [Green Giant Seasoned Steamers](http://greengiant.com/pages/SeasonedSteamersSubCategory.aspx) and a serving soon.  
![wpid-GreenGiantSteamersGifts-3-2012-12-13-07-30.jpg](/images/2012/2012-12/2012-12-13-gifts-from-jules-via-green-giant-img-3.jpg)

It’s a little hard to see them in the box, let me show you better… The bowl is perfect for all sorts of things; huge bowl of veggies, salads and even popcorn.

Isn’t the apron cute? I love all the veggies on it… here’s a close-up…

Jules talked about how much she loves her veggies… well, I, too, love veggies. I can’t wait to use my coupon and try the Green Giant Seasoned Steamers. I will be sure to let you know what I think.

Thanks, Jules and Green Giant for my goodies!
