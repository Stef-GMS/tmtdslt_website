I’ve often talked about how much I **HATE** our Oregon winters. I suffer from [Seasonal Affective Disorder](http://www.mayoclinic.com/health/seasonal-affective-disorder/DS00195) (SAD). The gray gloomy skies are just so hard to take. I have no idea how someone from the near-Arctic region survives.

I use daylight/full spectrum light bulbs in almost all of our lamps. It helps.

At my old job I had desk lamp which had daylight bulbs in it. At my current job, my desk used to be right near a window; it too helped. I sat there for four-and-a-half years. We’ve consolidated our office buildings and now I don’t sit near a window. It is soooooo damn dreary! I hate it. I don’t complain at work; it wouldn’t help and would only make things worse.

The sun has been making an appearance more in the last couple of days and it is WONDERFUL!!! 😀 _I’ve missed you, Mr. Sun!! Thank you for making our winter days more like spring!! My flower bulbs are enjoying you, too!_ 😉

When I walked downstairs this morning I saw it gleaming through the blinds. When I opened them I saw this…  
![wpid-IMG_0493-2012-02-5-12-55.jpg](/images/2012/2012-02/2012-02-05-giant-orb-in-the-sky-img-1.jpg)

It reminded me of when our dearly departed _Lady_ would lay in _Mr. Sun_. She absolutely loved it. If _Mr. Sun_ was peaking in the window, she’d find a way to absorb his rays.  
![wpid-LadyPlayingWithHotWheels01-2012-02-5-12-55.jpg](/images/2012/2012-02/2012-02-05-giant-orb-in-the-sky-img-2.jpg)

So I decided to lay down. Yep, on the floor and soak up some rays. This is what _The Offspring_ saw…  
![wpid-IMG_0502-2012-02-5-12-55.jpg](/images/2012/2012-02/2012-02-05-giant-orb-in-the-sky-img-3.jpg)

It was wonderful!! I highly recommend doing it. You’ll feel better!
