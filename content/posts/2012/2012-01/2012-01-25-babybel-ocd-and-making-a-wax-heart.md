I have a bit of obsessive compulsive disorder (OCD). Sometimes I can’t help myself; other times I really don’t care. It’s WEIRD!!! What is _really_ weird is I have “Babybel OCD”. _What’s that?_ you ask… It’s easier to show you…

Just about every time I eat a [Mini Babybel](http://www.thelaughingcow.com/products/mini-babybel/) cheese round this is my process… (NOTE: forgive the photos… crappy lighting at my work desk)  
![wpid-IMG_1800-2012-01-25-09-00.jpg](/images/2012/2012-01/2012-01-25-babybel-ocd-and-making-a-wax-heart-img-1.jpg)

I open the round just like you’re supposed to… pull the little _pulls_….  
![wpid-IMG_1801-2012-01-25-09-00.jpg](/images/2012/2012-01/2012-01-25-babybel-ocd-and-making-a-wax-heart-img-2.jpg)

…then I put the “cap” underneath the “bottom”…  
![wpid-IMG_1802-2012-01-25-09-00.jpg](/images/2012/2012-01/2012-01-25-babybel-ocd-and-making-a-wax-heart-img-3.jpg)

…and then eat the cheese… (guess it’s better than “cutting the cheese”… sorry… I couldn’t resist! 😉 )  
![wpid-IMG_1803-2012-01-25-09-00.jpg](/images/2012/2012-01/2012-01-25-babybel-ocd-and-making-a-wax-heart-img-4.jpg)

… put the pulls inside…  
![wpid-IMG_1804-2012-01-25-09-00.jpg](/images/2012/2012-01/2012-01-25-babybel-ocd-and-making-a-wax-heart-img-5.jpg)

…fold in front side…  
![wpid-IMG_1805-2012-01-25-09-00.jpg](/images/2012/2012-01/2012-01-25-babybel-ocd-and-making-a-wax-heart-img-6.jpg)

…fold in the back side…  
![wpid-IMG_1806-2012-01-25-09-00.jpg](/images/2012/2012-01/2012-01-25-babybel-ocd-and-making-a-wax-heart-img-7.jpg)

…fold the left edge in…  
![wpid-IMG_1807-2012-01-25-09-00.jpg](/images/2012/2012-01/2012-01-25-babybel-ocd-and-making-a-wax-heart-img-8.jpg)

… fold the right edge…  
![wpid-IMG_1808-2012-01-25-09-00.jpg](/images/2012/2012-01/2012-01-25-babybel-ocd-and-making-a-wax-heart-img-9.jpg)

…and _Voila!_ a Babybel wax heart… 😉  
![wpid-IMG_1809-2012-01-25-09-00.jpg](/images/2012/2012-01/2012-01-25-babybel-ocd-and-making-a-wax-heart-img-10.jpg)

It’s complusive… when I try to just toss it my stomach starts flip-flopping and I have been known to get it out of the trash and fold it! Then I wash-wash-wash my hands! UGH! I’m nuts, right? (_Rafael, you be nice!)_
