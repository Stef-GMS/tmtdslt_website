Don’t faint… three posts in two days… I’m not going for a record here, but I guess the [sunshine](http://TooMuchToDoSoLittleTime.com/2012/05/more-spring-happiness/) is helping… sort of. 😉

There is a downside to me enjoying the sunshine. Whenever I am in the sun, this happens…  
![wpid-Image-2012-05-8-13-00.jpg](/images/2012/2012-05/2012-05-08-downside-to-sunshine-img-1.jpg)

YEP… gross, right?!? You should see my neck! MAN IT ITCHES SOOOOO MUCH!

In addition to burning if I’m in the sun, I get a nasty ITCHY rash. You guessed it… I have [sun allergy](http://www.mayoclinic.com/health/sun-allergy/DS01178). It’s a Catch-22, right? I get depressed when the sun isn’t out, but if I’m out in the sun, I have a reaction. UGH!

I was sitting under an umbrella chair at _The Offspring’s_ softball game yesterday. I had a long sleeve T-Shirt on… good thing… or my arm would be itching, too!

I don’t care… I’ll take the SUN over rain … just need to keep from getting a rash. Any suggestions?
