First of all, YES I AM posting two blog posts on back-to-back days. And NO Hell didn’t freeze over last night…

As I mentioned [yesterday](http://TooMuchToDoSoLittleTime.com/2012/05/smile-sunny-blue-skies-and-a-dancing-flower/), I’m starting to come out of my funk… it’s amazing what sunshine does for my spirits. 😀

I had another surprise yesterday, but couldn’t get a picture until after I got home from _The Offspring’s_ softball game. A couple years ago, _Ma_ and _Pa_ gave _Hubby_ and me a lilac bush for our anniversary. _Ma_ remembered me mentioning that I had a lilac bush at the house where I grew up and how I missed it and how hers gave me special memories. Our anniversary is in August so last year was the first year it was really “in the ground”. It didn’t bloom last year, but …

Look what I saw yesterday!! Lots of pretty dark purple BLOOMS! It’s going to be sooooo beautiful as it grows! (it’s only about 22 inches tall right now) 😀
