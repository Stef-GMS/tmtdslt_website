Support this family and honor the memory of this brave young girl by wearing purple on Tuesday.

Maddie Lauer, 14, passed away Sunday from a rare form of thyroid cancer. Her favorite color was purple. Students from her schools are asking that everyone wear purple in her honor. They will be passing out purple ribbons in her honor at the front of her high school.
