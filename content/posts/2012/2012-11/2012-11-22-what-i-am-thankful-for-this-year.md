Thanksgiving is _Mama_’s “holiday”. Yes, she loves Christmas, but _Thanksgiving_ is a time to remember everything and everyone who have blessed our lives.

I thought I would give you a few things that I am thankful for this year…

Like many, I am thankful for my family. I really hate living so far away from my family, but thanks to the internet and cheap phone calls, I can stay in touch. For each of you, please know that you are not far from my thoughts even though you are far in distance.

My in-laws are so wonderful. We don’t get to see you as much as we’d like, but it’s great to see you when we do. I love this time a year because we get to see you twice in nearly a month! We truly hope that you can visit more. I know you’re busy and with our crazy schedule it is so hard for us to get to your place. (See you later today! 😉 )

Our “adoptive” family – _Ma_ and _Pa_… the two of you have welcomed us into your lives. I can’t believe we started out as neighbors nearly 17 years ago and now we’re family. I can’t imagine what our lives would be like without you in it.

My friends, thank you for being there when I need you; especially a few weeks ago during my _[Life Altering Event](http://toomuchtodosolittletime.com/2012/11/life-altering-event/)_.

Two of the people in my life I am most thankful for are _Hubby_ and _The Offspring_. You two are my life and I would be empty without you.

I’ve really missed _The Offspring_! She came home yesterday from college for their Thanksgiving Break. 😀 It’s soooo GREAT to see her! I don’t know if she knows really how much I have missed her.

It doesn’t seem like two months since we dropped her off at college. She seems to be doing great with life’s _[Training Wheels](http://toomuchtodosolittletime.com/2012/09/training-wheels/)_.

First I surprised her by running out the front door, camera in hand, and snapping a ton of photos… Most of them, OK all of them, are out of focus, so I decided to through this into a sequential collage… you can get how this went down… 😉  
![wpid-WelcomeHome-2012-11-22-08-30.jpg](/images/2012/2012-11/2012-11-22-what-i-am-thankful-for-this-year-img-2.jpg)

_The Offspring_ was a bit embarrassed, but her roommate Katie was sure happy to see me… well, she is my “BFFL”! At least that’s what each of our cell phones have next to one another’s names. _(**Update \[27-Nov\]:** “BFFL” = Best Friend For Life)_

Wanna know something amazing??? _The Offspring_ and Katie were randomly chosen as roommates. Not so odd; but the fact that they have been friends for over 9 years, even though they didn’t go to the same high school and played on the same travel softball team for 6 years when they were younger… that’s what’s so odd. Random… WOW!

Oooops… I got off track a bit there. Actually, no I didn’t…

I am SO thankful that _The Offspring_ has such a GREAT roommate and they have been able to reconnect and experience college together! _Happy Thanksgiving, Katie! 😀_

When _The Offspring_ arrived, she got a special surprise… when she walked in the door I had her favorite Thanksgiving recipe in the oven… my pumpkin bread. I have to admit… it’s awesome! I receive lots of requests to make it this time of year. In fact, _The Offspring_ is taking some back to college for her roommate and friends across the hall. She told them that it was even better than my [Zucchini Bread](http://toomuchtodosolittletime.com/2012/11/stefs-zucchini-bread/) they had last month. They can’t wait! 😀

I thought I would share the recipe with it with you on this Thanksgiving, but since so many of you are buried with Thanksgiving today I thought I would put it off for a few days. I promise I will share!

Happy Thanksgiving!

_For what are you thankful for this year?_
