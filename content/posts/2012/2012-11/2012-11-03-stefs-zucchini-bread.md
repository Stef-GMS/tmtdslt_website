Stef’s Zucchini Bread  
———————————————————  
Serving Size: 20 slices  
Yield: 2 large loaves

Ingredients:

3 whole eggs  
1 Cup oil  
2 tsp vanilla  
2 cups sugar  
2 cups zucchini, [grated](http://toomuchtodosolittletime.com/2012/10/zucchini-coming-out-my-ears/)  
3 cups flour  
1 teaspoon kosher salt  
3 teaspoons cinnamon  
1 teaspoon baking soda  
1/4 teaspoon baking powder

Directions:

1\. Preheat oven to 325.

2\. Grease 2 loaf pans well.

3\. Beat eggs, oil, sugar and vanilla. Mix Well.

4\. Add grated zucchini and dry ingredients. Mix well.

5\. Pour mixture into the two well greased loaf pans.

6\. Bake at 325 degrees F for 60-65 minutes. Test for doneness at 60 minutes and then every 5 minutes until done. (Done is when a toothpick is inserted in middle and it comes out clean.)
