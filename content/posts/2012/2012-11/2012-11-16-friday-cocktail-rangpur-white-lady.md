![](/images/2012/2012-11/2012-11-16-friday-cocktail-rangpur-white-lady-img-1.png "wpid-FridayCocktail-2012-11-9-07-30.png")I think I’ve mentioned that I have a “page-a-day” cocktail calendar. Well, back in July there as a drink listed. It sounded good, but I never got around to making it… until now… sort of. I’ve changed one of the ingredients a bit and I am SOOOO glad I did.

According to my calendar, the _White Lady_ was created in London back in 1919.

Well, I’m not a big gin drinker. I suppose that’s why I never made this drink before. Gin is too dry for me and not really my “cup of tea”… er, liquor. 😉

I have two different kinds of gin in the house. One is _London Dry Gin_ and the other is _Tanqueray’s Rangpur Gin_. The Rangpur has been infused with Rangpur limes. I like limes so I thought let’s mix things up a bit.

Cast of Characters: _[Tanqueray Rangpur Gin](http://www.tanqueray.com/product.html)_, _[Cointreau](http://www.cointreau.us/)_ (orange liqueur), fresh lemon and ice (not pictured)  
![wpid-RangpurWhiteLadyCast-2012-11-16-19-43.jpg](/images/2012/2012-11/2012-11-16-friday-cocktail-rangpur-white-lady-img-2.jpg)

Put glass in freezer to get it really good and cold – I put mine in for 15 minutes.

Put ice is a shaker. As you can see, I used crushed ice.  
![wpid-CrushedIce-2012-11-16-19-43.jpg](/images/2012/2012-11/2012-11-16-friday-cocktail-rangpur-white-lady-img-3.jpg)

Pour two jiggers of gin over the ice…  
![wpid-2jiggersGin-2012-11-16-19-43.jpg](/images/2012/2012-11/2012-11-16-friday-cocktail-rangpur-white-lady-img-4.jpg)

Add 1 jigger of freshly squeezed lemon juice…  
![wpid-1jiggersLemon-2012-11-16-19-43.jpg](/images/2012/2012-11/2012-11-16-friday-cocktail-rangpur-white-lady-img-5.jpg)

Add 1 jigger of Cointreau – do not spill it because it’s expensive and most especially when it’s the last of it in the bottle! — _K8, can you believe I spilled it?!?! (YES, I was SOOO tempted to lick it off the counter!!)_  
![wpid-1jiggerCointreau-2012-11-16-19-43.jpg](/images/2012/2012-11/2012-11-16-friday-cocktail-rangpur-white-lady-img-6.jpg)

Remove glass from freezer… (yes, this is a wine glass, but who cares among us friends! 😉 )  
![wpid-WellChilledGlass-2012-11-16-19-43.jpg](/images/2012/2012-11/2012-11-16-friday-cocktail-rangpur-white-lady-img-7.jpg)

Put lid on shaker and shake vigorously and then strain into chilled glass. Garnish with a lemon mini-wedge.  
![wpid-RangpurWhiteLady-2012-11-16-19-43.jpg](/images/2012/2012-11/2012-11-16-friday-cocktail-rangpur-white-lady-img-8.jpg)

Now, as I said, I’m not a big gin drinker… This is really good… I bet it’s the fresh lemon and Cointreau that made it so good… oh and the Rangpur limes! 😉

Enjoy!

_Have you ever had a White Lady?_

Rangpur White Lady  
—————————————  
Serving Size: 1 glass

Well chilled glass  
Ice, crushed  
2 jiggers Tanqueray Rangpur Gin  
1 jigger Cointreau  
1 jigger Freshly Squeezed Lemon Juice

Pour all ingredients to shaker and shake vigorously. Strain into well chilled glass and garnish with with a mini lemon wedge.
