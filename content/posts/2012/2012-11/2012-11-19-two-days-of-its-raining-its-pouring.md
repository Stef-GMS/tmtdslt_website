My friend Kim lives out in Vernonia. I hope they’re OK!!! They had two 500-year floods in less than 11 years; the whole town flooded in 2009. She posted on Facebook that the Nehalem River is over it’s bank just down the road from her!!!! The Nehalem River is the northern boundry for their property!! YIKES! _Pray for her and her family and friends!!!!

I feel like a drowned rat and I’m indoors… Heading out soonish… maybe! _Ma_ just called and told us to not leave the house. I do have to walk up to the mailbox at least.

We aren’t near a river or anything, so we shouldn’t have any flooding at our house. We’re at the bottom of a hill, but there’s a storm drain near us.
