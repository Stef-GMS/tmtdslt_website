I am trying to have an upbeat outlook on life. As I’ve mentioned recently, things have been kind of crappy. Over the weekend I did several things that were fun and I think that helped me have a smile on my face this morning. 😀

While many are busy watching basketball playoffs, I took an alternate route… Movies… I LOVE MOVIES!

First, Friday evening, after _The Offspring’s_ softball game, I met up with a friend and we went to see _[The Raven](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1486192/)_. It was the first time my friend _T_ and I had gone out – aside from work events. It was fun! We had dinner at the theatre, which was pretty tasty.

On Saturday, _Hubby_ and I went to see _[The Five-Year Engagement](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1195478/)_. Afterwards, we went to have an “appetizer” for dinner (we weren’t terribly hungry, so we split and appetizer). When we got home, we all watched _[The Incredible Hulk (2008)](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0800080/)._

Sunday was a relaxing day. I started watching _[Captain America: The First Avenger (2011)](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0458339/)_ in 3D, but didn’t get it finished because we had company coming over, but plan on finishing it tonight (after _The Offspring’s_ game).

With _[The Avengers](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0848228/)_ coming out this Friday, I want to watch all the individual movies beforehand. We watched [Iron Man](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0371746/) last week. I have some serious catching up to do before FRIDAY!

I think having only one under my “[Movie Reviews](http://TooMuchToDoSoLittleTime.com/category/movie-reviews/)” category is kind of silly, so I plan on writing another movie reviews for all these… that should keep me busy for a while!

Also coming “soon” is my birthday “reveal”. _Hubby_ and _The Offspring_ surprised me with some nice things for my birthday. 😀
