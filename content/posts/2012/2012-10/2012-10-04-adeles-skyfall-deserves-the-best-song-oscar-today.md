Whether or not you are an Adele fan (like me) or a James Bond fan (like me) you HAVE to watch this video of Adele’s new song. It’s called _[Skyfall](http://itunes.apple.com/us/album/skyfall-single/id566322358)_ and is the theme song for the new James Bond film _[Skyfall](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1074638/)_ starring [Daniel Craig](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0185819/) as _007,_ [Helen McCrory](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0567031/) as _Clair Dowar_, [Ralph Fiennes](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0000146/) as Gareth Mallory, [Javier Bardem](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0000849/) and Raoul Silva, [Ben Whishaw](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0924210/) and the new _Q_ and of course _[Dame Judi Dench](http://www.imdb.com/name/nm0001132/)_ and _M_.

The song _Skyfall_ is available to download on _[iTunes](http://itunes.apple.com/us/album/skyfall-single/id566322358)_ now and [Amazon](http://www.amazon.com/Skyfall/dp/B009KUAOH0/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8) tomorrow (Oct 5).

The movie/film _Skyfall_ is scheduled to premiere in London on Oct 23 and is released here in the US and Canada on Nov 9 – _Damn… I need to buy a ticket to London!_ 😉 If you live in another country, click [here](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt1074638/releaseinfo) to get your release date.

This video has the lyrics and is sooo cool to watch (and listen to as well).  

OK… I’ll let you go now… so you can listen to the song over and over and over again! (like me) 😉

_Isn’t Adele’s Skyfall just AMAZING!?! She has “my” vote for Best Original Song!_
