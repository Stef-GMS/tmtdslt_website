Just fixed my broken 22 year old Krups 964 Espresso Maker! The “O ring” was out of its track. I just unscrewed the metal filter on the underneath side and removed the metal filter. Then removed the “O ring”. Cleaned it all out and _Viola!_ It’s working again!

Too bad I’m feeling better from being sick… Have to clean the house. So much for bliss… 🙁

On second thought… I wouldn’t want to overdo it… right? 😉
